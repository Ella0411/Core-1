The Eiffel Tower is truly filled with romance.
Many people spend time with their families, lovers, and friends while gazing at the Eiffel Tower. Although it can be uncomfortable right under the tower due to aggressive vendors, it looks truly beautiful from afar.
The Arc de Triomphe is an impressive structure, admired for its incredible detail and grandeur. I’ve heard that it’s possible to go inside and climb to the top, though I haven’t done it myself.
Additionally, the way the roads converge around the Arc de Triomphe is truly spectacular.
